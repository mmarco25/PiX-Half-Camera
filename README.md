# PiX-Half-Camera
DIY Half Frame digital (vintage) camera, in short a FinePix (still Fuji :) ) vintageDigitalCamera, together with a RaspberryPi

What if I want to shot Half Frame in digital, but do not want to spend 1000£/$/€ on a new camera (need to keep my GAS at large somehow)?

Enter the PiX Half Camera. In short a FinePix (still Fuji :) ) vintageDigitalCamera, together with a RaspberryPi (or a linux PC in a first installment for what it worth).

The workflow is basically: Shooting the FinePix 40i invertical mode and once the card is full (a 128MB SmartMedia card could store about 70-72 at the max resolution of 1800x2400 Fine), once finished I connect the camera (Via USB cable) to the PC (or a small box with a Raspi inside) and after the pictures have been downloaded I can select via a script how i want the pictures.

Notes:

All the pictures (about 36 files) will be dypthics (but original portrait single pictures will be stored anyway as an option)

Available selections (for now):

* color vs BW
* date imprinted vs no date ( 1 )
* effects can be added (supported for now grain/Expired film, Halation) 
* other can be added

A film strip and or Contact sheet will be generated (to be selected) 
( 1 ) dates is take straight from the EXIF data



All pictures are taken with Fujifilm FinePix 40i
